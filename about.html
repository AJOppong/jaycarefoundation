Who We Are

A community-driven foundation dedicated to improving lives across the Ahafo Region of Ghana.

Our Story

Jay's Care Foundation is a community-driven, non-profit organization established to serve vulnerable populations across the Ahafo Region of Ghana — particularly widows, the elderly, women, youth, and underserved communities.

The Foundation was born out of a deep recognition that communities in the Ahafo Region continue to face significant socioeconomic and healthcare challenges — from limited access to health services, to high youth unemployment, to inadequate support for widows and disaster-affected individuals.

We believe in structured, sustainable, and community-based interventions — not just charity. Our work spans free health screenings, widow and elderly support, feeding the homeless, girl child education, disaster relief, and social entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

To improve lives through healthcare outreach, social support programs, and sustainable community development initiatives.

Our Vision

To become a leading humanitarian foundation in Ghana, transforming lives and building resilient communities.
