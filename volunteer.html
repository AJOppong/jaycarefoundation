Become a Volunteer

Jay's Care Foundation relies on the passion and dedication of volunteers to deliver our programs across the six districts of the Ahafo Region. Whether you are a healthcare professional, educator, or community organizer, there is a meaningful role for you here.

Volunteers support our healthcare outreach screenings, Feed the Street initiative, Girl Child Support Program, widow and elderly visits, and disaster relief responses.

Partnerships & Donations

We welcome collaboration from donors, corporate organizations, NGOs, and government institutions. Support may be in the form of financial contributions, in-kind donations, volunteer services, or strategic partnerships.
