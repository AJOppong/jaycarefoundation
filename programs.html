Our Programs

Six structured intervention programs delivering healthcare, social support, and empowerment across the Ahafo Region of Ghana.

Healthcare Outreach Programs

Free general health screenings (blood pressure, diabetes, eye screening), breast cancer screening and awareness, and support for individuals affected by health conditions — delivered in collaboration with qualified nurses and medical doctors.

Support Healthcare

Widow & Elderly Support

Annual support programs during Mother's Day and Father's Day, providing food items, clothing, and financial assistance. We also deliver emotional and community support interventions to widows and the elderly.

Support This Program

Feed the Street Initiative

Organized during festive seasons, this initiative distributes cooked meals and essential items to the homeless. We are committed to promoting their dignity and fostering social inclusion in our communities.

Support This Initiative

Girl Child Support Program

Annual distribution of sanitary pads to selected schools, accompanied by menstrual hygiene education. This program directly reduces absenteeism among schoolgirls and keeps them in school.

Support Girls' Education

Disaster Relief Support

Emergency response to disaster-affected communities, providing relief items such as food, clothing, and basic supplies. We also support the recovery and reintegration of affected families.

Donate to Relief

Social Entrepreneurship Program

Skills development and vocational training, startup support for small businesses, and promotion of economic independence. We empower youth to create sustainable livelihoods for themselves and their communities.

Empower Youth
